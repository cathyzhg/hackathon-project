How Our Project Helps

Our project helps people clearly see how urban growth affects wildlife over time. By turning data into simple graphs and a website, we make the issue easier to understand. When people can see the changes visually, they are more likely to
care and take action. We hope our work encourages smarter planning that protects wildlife while cities continue to grow.
