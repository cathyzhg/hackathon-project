The Solution

Our solution transforms complex urban data into a simple, interactive experience that reveals how city development affects wildlife. By calculating a Habitat Vitality Index from factors like green space, roads, and population density, we show which animals can still survive and provide visual storytelling and actionable tools to help people take small steps toward wildlife-friendly cities. We envision a future where cities are designed for both people and wildlife, where planning balances ecosystems with infrastructure, and where communities understand the impact of their choices and work together to restore biodiversity and build healthier, more sustainable urban environments.
